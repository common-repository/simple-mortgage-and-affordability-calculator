Let your website visitors calculate their loan and mortgage affordability with this Mortgage and Affordability Calculator(plugin + widget)

== Description ==  
### Simple Affordability Calculator Features: ###

- Find out how much they can afford spending per month on their mortgage payment.  
- Figure out what is the best budget for buying a property and start planning for their mortgage.  
- Enter their desired monthly mortgage amount, interest rate and mortgage length in years and calculate how much they are about to pay in total, how high is the interest paid and what is the maximum loan they can ask in order to afford it.  
- Select "Calculate" and wait a few seconds for the results to appear.

Plugin can be used inside any article by placing the following code:<br />
[affordability-calc]<br />
If you want to use it as a widget just slide the "Affordability Calculator" widget to your desired sidebar.<br />
  
### [Demo](http://wpcream.com/demo/affordability-calculator.php)  ###

== Installation ==

1. Extract zip file and then upload it under /wp-content/plugins/ through ftp.  
1. Log into your Wordpress admin section and enable Plugin.  
1. If you want to use the widget as well just visit the Widget settings under the Appearance section.  

== Usage ==
Plugin can be used inside any article by placing the following code:
[affordability-calc]
If you want to use it as a widget just slide the "Affordability Calculator" widget to your desired sidebar.
